COGNITION SUPPORT

Have a sharp cognitive function and eliminate dementia risk!

Nutrition for the Brain

Omega-3 fatty acids, antioxidants, and B-vitamins are essential for brain health. Sources include fish, nuts, seeds, whole grains, and leafy greens.

Healthy Lifestyle

Adequate sleep, physical activity, and lifelong learning improve cognitive function. Meditation and mindfulness also enhance memory and focus.

Mental Stimulation

Activities such as reading, puzzles, and learning new skills help maintain cognitive performance and protect against decline.
