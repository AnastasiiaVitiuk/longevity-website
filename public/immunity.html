IMMUNITY SUPPORT

Bolster your immune system and gain confidence when going through the world!

Nutrition and Micronutrients

A balanced diet provides the building blocks for immune cells. Vitamin C, Vitamin D, and Zinc are especially important for immune defense. Sources include fruits, vegetables, fortified foods, and sunlight exposure.

Healthy Lifestyle

Good sleep, regular physical activity, and stress management improve immune responses. Chronic stress and lack of rest can reduce the body’s ability to fight infections.

Gut Microbiome

A large part of immunity is linked to gut health. Fiber-rich foods and fermented products (like yogurt, kefir, sauerkraut) support a diverse microbiome, which in turn helps immune regulation.
